About Us

Roadrunner Swimming is a student organization at The University of Texas at San Antonio. We compete around Texas and carry the name UTSA along with us. Despite the competition we embrace, we are also a club composed of friendly, responsible and active teammates. We support each other at all times and look forward to improve our skills, meet new people and stay healthy! Feel free to browse around to find out more!

Membership

Roadrunner Swimming invites everyone to join! Whether you have swim experience or you are just starting out, we always encourage people to join us! There are only a couple of requirments to meet:

  • Must be UTSA student/Faculty/staff or an alumni
  • An overall GPA of 2.0 (3.0 for grad students)
  • Demonstrate proficient swimming skills in water deeper than 7 feet. New members must be able to pass a simple swim test which consists of completing a 50 yard freestyle without touching the floor of the pool.

All you would need to bring to each practice would be a suit, goggles, and a cap if needed. Though competing is not required, it is highly encouraged!

We always accept new members at any time during the season!

Membership Dues

There is a $75 membership fee in order to join the club, which includes:

  • Team T-shirt
  • Team Jacket
  • Team Swim Cap
  • Pool usage
  • Equipment usage
  • Transportation to meets

Fees must be paid to the Treasurer, see About
