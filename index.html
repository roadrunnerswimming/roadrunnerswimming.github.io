About Us

Roadrunner Swimming is a student led organization at The University of Texas at San Antonio. The purpose of our organization is to bring together swimmers to socialize, excercis, train, and participate in the sport of competitive swimming.

Membership

Roadrunner Swimming invites everyone to join! Whether you have swim experience or you are just starting out, we always encourage people to join us! There are only a couple of requirements to meet:

  • Must be UTSA student/Faculty/staff or an alumni
  • Have an overall GPA of 2.0 (3.0 for grad students)
  • Be able to swim 50 yards without touching the floor, lane ropes, or wall.

We always accept new members at any time during the semester!

Membership Dues

There is a $100 membership fee in order to join the club, which includes:

  • Team T-shirt
  • Team Jacket
  • Team Swim Cap
  • Meet Fees (Hotel/Gas/Entry Fees)
  • Equipment Usage
  • Coaching Fees
  • Social Events

Dues must be paid to the Treasurer within the first two weeks of practice, see About to find the Treasurer

FAQ

How do I join the team?

To join the team, contact us through utsaswimclub@gmail.com, facebook, or stop by one of our practices and find an officer. See About to meet the officers.

What do I bring to practice?

Your swimsuit, googles, and cap (if needed).

Is this club competitive?

Our team competes in 2-4 meets per semester in Texas and surrounding areas. Meets are not mandatory, however, we highly encourage our members to participate in meets whether it be by swimming or cheering on the team.
