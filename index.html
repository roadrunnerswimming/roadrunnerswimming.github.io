About Us

Roadrunner Swimming is a student led organization at The University of Texas at San Antonio. The purpose of our organization is to bring together swimmers to socialize, exercise, train, and participate in the sport of competitive swimming.

Membership

We are always accepting new members!

To be a member you must meet the following criteria:

  • To be eligible for membership, candidates must be enrolled, full-time undergraduate or graduate students at The University of Texas at San Antonio.
  • Mantaining an overall GPA of 2.0 is required for membership (3.0 for graduate students).
  • To swim, members must demonstrate proficient swimming skills in water deeper than 7 feet. Members must be able to pass a swim test which consists of completing a 50 yard swim without touching the floor or walls of the pool.

Membership Dues

Fall 2017 dues are $100 and include:

  • Team T-shirt
  • Team Jacket
  • Team Swim Cap
  • Meet Fees (Hotel/Gas/Entry Fees)
  • Equipment Usage
  • Coaching Fees
  • Social Events

Dues must be paid to the Treasurer within the first two weeks of practice, see About to find the Treasurer

FAQ

How do I join the team?

To join the team, contact us through utsaswimclub@gmail.com, facebook, or stop by one of our practices and find an officer. See About to meet the officers.

What do I bring to practice?

Your swimsuit, goggles, and cap (if needed).

Is this club competitive?

Our team competes in 2-4 meets per semester in Texas and surrounding areas. Meets are not mandatory, however, we highly encourage our members to participate in meets whether it be by swimming or cheering on the team.
